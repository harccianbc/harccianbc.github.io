Accessibility Statement

Institutional Compliance & Inclusion

Harccian™ is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. Our objective is to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA to provide an inclusive and equitable learning environment.

🖼️ Alt-Text for All AI Media
⌨️ Full Keyboard Navigation
🌓 High-Contrast Mode Support
📜 Transcript & Text Alternatives

While we strive for comprehensive accessibility, some areas of our platform may still have limitations due to the nature of AI-generated content and ongoing development. We are actively working to improve accessibility and welcome feedback from users.

If you encounter any difficulties accessing content on this website, or require assistance in any format (such as large print, audio, or alternative formats), please contact us directly. We will respond promptly and work with you to provide the necessary accommodations.
