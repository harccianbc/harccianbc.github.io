THE HARCCIAN FRATERNITIES

Unity • Discipline • Stewardship

WHY FRATERNITY?

Upon acceptance into Harccian Bible College, students may petition for entry into a virtual fraternity. These are inclusive, values-based guilds built on respect, character, and commitment to the Harccian standard.

We are not a secret society. Harccian fraternities are open, faith-centered, and values-driven. We honor Chirat as the head of Harccian Bible College and recognize his leadership over the institution.

FRATERNITY PROTOCOLS

  • LEADERSHIP: Each fraternity has a designated leader who oversees the process.
  • INCLUSION: All students may apply if they respect the Harccian standard.
  • SELECTION: After choosing a fraternity, the applicant must pass that fraternity's test.
  • FINALITY: Test results determine acceptance for the chosen fraternity.
