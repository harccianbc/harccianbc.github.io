The Manifesto

Harccian™ exists to provide honest, transparent, and genuinely free Bible and ministry training to those who seek the Truth — with no hidden costs, no bait-and-switch, and no deception.

We believe access to biblical education should be absolute. What we say is free, is free. What is not, will be declared with total clarity, upfront — always.

Our courses are strictly non-accredited. We offer spiritual growth and biblical knowledge. We will never misrepresent our status to the world.
