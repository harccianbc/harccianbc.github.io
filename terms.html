Harccian Bible College Privacy Decree

Effective April 2026
“Fides et Excellentia”

1. Information & Collection

Harccian Bible College (HBC) collects essential data only. This includes your Full Name, Email Address, and Chapter location. We collect this via Google Forms, direct email, and voluntary submissions.

Strict Exclusion: HBC DO NOT collect financial details, ID numbers, or health data.

The Ironclad Rule Your information will NEVER be sold or shared outside of HBC. Data is stored securely via Google Services with staff access limited by Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

2. Data Retention & Your Rights

Data is retained only while you are an active student. Alumni data is kept only with explicit consent. You maintain the following rights:

  • Access your HBC records at any time.
  • Correct inaccuracies immediately.
  • Request total deletion (Withdrawal of Consent).

3. Volunteers & Chapter Leaders

All HBC volunteers and leaders are bound by this decree. They must:

  • Maintain absolute confidentiality of student data.
  • Collect only essential information.
  • Ensure secure storage of any local HBC records.

*Breaches of these protocols will result in immediate removal of responsibilities.*

4. Visual Consent (Graduation)

Sharing achievements is completely voluntary. HBC only publishes photos or names with this specific permission:

“I give permission for Harccian Bible College to share my graduation photo, certificate, or achievements...”
