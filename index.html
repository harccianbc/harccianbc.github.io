Our History

The mission was tested many years ago in India, where we provided curriculum and certificates to dedicated pastors in the field.

The vision for this **Online Bible College** was birthed in 2024, creating a digital bridge to non-accredited elite theological training for the modern era.

Why Harccian?

Harccian exists to fill the gap in faith-based education with elite courses built on Scripture and calling rather than bureaucracy.

Not everyone earns a Harccian certificate. Our workload is deliberately challenging to ensure completion carries genuine weight.
