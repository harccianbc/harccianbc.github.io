HARCCIAN BIBLE COLLEGE - APPLICATION

Welcome to the Harccian™ Bible College Application Process

All information submitted must be accurate and truthful. Harccian™ Bible College assumes no responsibility for any complications arising from incorrect or misleading details. The information you provide will be used solely for the purpose of processing your application and issuing certificates where applicable.

Entry into Harccian™ Bible College is not designed for the careless or casual. We seek applicants who demonstrate diligence, integrity, and a commitment to excellence. While our programs are non-accredited, we uphold high standards for all who apply and study with us.

Entrance Requirements

This assessment requires a 90% pass mark.

If you do not meet the entrance requirement, PLEASE TRY AGAIN. We allow unlimited attempts for those dedicated to joining our college.

Assessment Structure

This entrance assessment consists of four (4) sections:

  • Administration
  • Bible Knowledge – You may reference your Bible to assist with answers
  • English Grammar – Assessing comprehension, clarity, and accuracy
  • Harccian Knowledge – Based on information found on this website

Important Notice

We strongly recommend completing this application in one sitting.

While we believe the system may include an autosave feature, this cannot be guaranteed at all times. As this is a large assessment, please ensure you proceed carefully and avoid leaving the application for extended periods to prevent any potential loss of progress.
