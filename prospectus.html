Welcome to Harccian Bible College

Harccian Bible College is a virtual, non-accredited, and non-registered training organization. We provide elite theological education online for students worldwide seeking deep spiritual growth without the constraints of traditional accreditation.

What Makes Us Unique

We operate outside the traditional accreditation system. Our courses are designed to foster genuine spiritual and theological development for dedicated learners globally, regardless of location or socioeconomic background.

Our Mission & Philosophy

Fides et Excellentia — Faith and Excellence. We aim to cultivate serious biblical study and spiritual growth through accessible online courses rooted in Scripture. Financial status should never hinder a calling.

Our History

The mission began in India, providing curriculum and certificates to dedicated pastors. The vision for this online college was born in 2024, creating a digital bridge for elite, non-accredited theological training designed for the modern era.

Why Choose Harccian?

We fill a vital gap in faith-based education with courses built on Scripture and calling, not bureaucracy. Our coursework is challenging to ensure certificates carry weight and value. Applicants must be over 18.
