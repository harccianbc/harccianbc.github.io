Harccian Bible College

Prospectus 2026

Welcome to Harccian Bible College — a virtual, non-accredited, and non-registered training organization. We offer elite theological education online for students worldwide seeking deep spiritual growth without the constraints of traditional accreditation.

Our curriculum is rooted in Scripture and calling, designed for serious learners who desire flexible, accessible biblical studies. We are committed to integrity, excellence, and spiritual depth.

What Sets Us Apart

Harccian Bible College is a **virtual college** operating outside the accreditation system. We are not a registered training organization, but our courses aim to foster genuine spiritual and theological development for committed students worldwide.

Whether you're in a remote area or seeking flexible online study, our programs are designed for accessibility—regardless of socioeconomic background or location.

Our Mission & Philosophy

Fides et Excellentia — Faith and Excellence. We aim to cultivate serious biblical study and spiritual growth through accessible online courses rooted in Scripture.

We believe that **financial status** should never hinder a calling. We welcome students over 18 from all backgrounds. Our challenging coursework ensures that certificates carry real weight and value.
